[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows falling across the wooden floor
I’ve been waiting to walk you through that door
There's a fire burning, steady and bright
We’re leaving the heavy world outside tonight

[Verse 1]
The dusty road fades in the rearview mirror
With every whisper I just draw you nearer
We chased this feeling through the lonely years
Now your soft hand is calming all my fears

[Pre-Chorus]
I can see the longing painted in your eyes
Underneath the canvas of the midnight skies
Let our bodies talk in a slow, deep dance
I am giving in to this wild romance

[Chorus]
Heart to heart, where the shadows play
I’ll hold you close until the break of day
Soft and easy, the way you move with me
Lost in the warmth of our own chemistry
